ICE HOCKEY

Last Update: 15/12/2017

General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • For penalty shootouts, the winner will have 1 goal added to their score at the completion of the shoot out to determine the final score of the game.
  • For period betting, the specific period must be played in its entirety for bets to stand, unless explicitly stated below or in the individual Bet Type rules. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
  • 3rd Period betting does not include overtime or penalty shootouts.
  • If the game starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the game commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the game commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.
  • Overtime does not count unless otherwise stated.
Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will win the game.

1 X 2

  • Predict who will win the game / period. This market will contain the two teams and the draw as betting selections. Overtime does not count.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Handicap (In-Play)

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Goals: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line for the game/ period.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential goals have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Goals: Over / Under (In-Play)

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line for the game/ period.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential goals have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Goals: Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of goals will be odd or even for the game/ period.

Team to Score First

  • Predict which team will score first in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and a team has already been scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If neither team has scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.

Team to Score Last

  • Predict which team will score last in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.

Team with the Highest Scoring Period

  • Predict which team will score the most in a single period.
  • Overtime does not count.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 3 periods of normal play, then bets are considered void.
