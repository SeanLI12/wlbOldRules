MOTOR SPORT

Last Update: 18/06/2020

General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be void.
  • Podium positions will count as the official results, regardless of any subsequent disqualification or amendment to the result.
  • Bets will be settled based on the driver / rider ranking after the specified race according to the official FIA / FIM results.
Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict which driver or team will win the race.

Head to Head Markets

  • If one of the two drivers / riders do not start the race, all bets will be void.
  • Bets will be settled based upon one driver's / rider's ranking against the other driver / rider after the specified race.
  • If both drivers / riders fail to finish a race, then bets will be void.
  • If both drivers / riders start a specific race and only one fails to finish, then the driver / rider who completes the race will be deemed the winner.
  • Head to Head (Constructor) will be settled based upon which constructor has the best ranked car in the final result.

Fastest Lap

  • Predict which driver will record the fastest lap in the specific race.
  • Qualifying and practice laps do not count.
  • The lap time in milliseconds is valid for settlement purposes.

  • Predict which driver will record the fastest lap in the specified lap or cluster of laps.
  • The lap time in milliseconds is valid for settlement purposes.

Number of Classified Finishers

  • Predict how many drivers / riders will complete at least 90% of the race and be regarded as classified finishers by FIA classification.

Safety Car

  • Predict whether or not the Safety Car will be used in the race.
  • If the race starts under Safety Car conditions, all bets will be voided.
  • VSC (Virtual Safety Car) will not be regarded as a Safety Car.

Top X

  • Predict the first X placed drivers / riders in the specific race according to classified position based on the official FIA / FIM results.
  • Podium positions are final and any change of result after the race will not count towards settlement.

Winning Margin

  • Predict the time difference between the winning driver / rider and the runner-up at the end of the race.

Winning Constructor

  • Predict which team will have the winning car in the specific race.

Top X Constructors

  • Predict which teams will be ranked in the listed places for the specific race.
  • Results will be based on the actual standing regardless of repetition of the teams.

Retirement Markets

  • Predict which driver or team will retire in the order listed. 
  • A car is considered retired for settlement purposes if it does not pass the finish line when the session or race is considered as completed, unless it is disqualified.  The session for a specific driver can be considered as completed in the lap when the chequered flag is waved to signal the end of the race.
  • If more than 1 driver or team retires in the same lap where the first retirement happened, dead-heat rules will apply.
  • If a driver or car retires in the pit or pit lane, the last started lap is c for settlement purposes.

Lap X Total Retirements

  • Predict whether the total retirements for the lap stated will be over or under the value indicated. 

Pit Stop Markets

  • Predict which driver or team will enter the pit-lane in the order listed.
  • If a car enters the pit-lane and retires, it will still be considered as a pit stop for settlement purpose.
  • Markets may also be offered in the form of Pit Stops to occur over or under the lap stated.
  • In case of race abandonment, all bets will be considered void unless the market has been already settled or unconditionally determined.

Overtaking Markets

  • Predict the total overtakings made by a specific driver, specific team or all cars within period listed.
  • Any overtaking made during the 1st lap is not considered for settlement purposes.
  • Any overtaking need to be maintained until the end of the lap in order to be considered valid for settlement purposes.
  • Any overtaking of a specific driver in the same lap when entering or exiting the pit is not considered for settlement purposes.
  • Overtaking a car in the lap of retirement is not considered for settlement purposes.
  • Lapping and Unlapping is not considered as overtaking.
  • Team total overtakings is settled based on the accumulated number of overtakings by both cars in the race. 
  • In case of race abandonment, all bets will be considered void unless the market has been already settled or unconditionally determined.

Total Finishers

  • Predict whether the total number of finishing drivers will be over or under the value indicated. 
  • A driver is considered as finished when the pass the finishing line for the session or race stated. The session for a specific driver can be considered as completed in the lap when the chequered flag is waved to signal the end of the race. 
  • In case of race abandonment or premature race end, all bets will be considered void.

Grid Position of Winner

  • Predict the starting position of the specified race winner.
  • This market may be offered as single position or range of positions.

Group Winner

  • Predict the highest placing driver or team at the time of podium presentation. Drivers are grouped together for betting purpose.
  • If all drivers or teams in the selected group failed to be classified, those completing the most laps will be deemed the winner. If there are more than 2 winners, dead heat rules applied.
  • If all drivers or teams in the selected group retire in the same lap, then bets will be void.
  • Non-Runner no bet. If a driver in the group retires in or before formation lap, all the bets on this market will be void.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the driver's / rider's final position will be an odd or even number
  • If the driver / rider fails to complete the race, all bets will be void.
