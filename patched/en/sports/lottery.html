LOTTERY BETTING

Last Update: 29/04/2021

General Rules
  • All Lottery bets will be settled according to results declared by the respective Lottery organizations conducting the draw.
  • Betting will close 1 hour or earlier before the official draw time declared by the respective Lottery organizations conducting the draw.
  • If a draw takes place before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the draw commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the draw commenced will be considered void.
Lottery Draws
  • The Hong Kong Lottery is based on the Mark Six Lotto game conducted by the Hong Kong Lotteries Board. 6 balls + 1 bonus ball are drawn from a total of 49.
  • The Irish Lottery is based on the Irish National Lottery main draw (Irish Lotto). 6 balls + 1 bonus ball are drawn from a total of 47.
  • China Lottery:
    • The China Lottery is based on the China Welfare Lottery's Union Lotto draw. 6 red balls are drawn from a total of 33. 1 blue ball is drawn from a total of 16.
    • China Welfare Lottery 3D is a set-prize numbers game played seven days a week. Customers choose a three-digit number from 000 to 999.
    • 7-Luck Lottery is another type of welfare lottery issued by China's Welfare Lottery Center. 7 balls + 1 blue ball are drawn from a total of 30.
    • Orient 6+1 Lottery is also another type of welfare lottery issued by China's Welfare Lottery Center. 6 balls + 1 zodiac ball are drawn from a total of 12 based on the Chinese Zodiac.
  • The Korean Lottery is based on the Korea National Lottery main draw (NLotto). 6 balls + 1 bonus ball are drawn from a total of 45.
  • Vietnam Lottery:
    • The Vietnam Lottery is based on Mega 6/45 Lotto game and Power 6/55 Lotto game conducted by the Vietnam Lottery (Vietlott).
    • Mega 6/45 Lotto have 6 balls are drawn from a total of 45.
    • Power 6/55 Lotto have 6 balls + 1 bonus ball are drawn from a total of 55.
  • USA Lottery:
    • The USA Lottery is based on Mega Millions and Powerball conducted by all American lotteries.
    • Mega Millions have 5 balls are drawn from a total of 70 + 1 Mega Ball are drawn from a total of 25.
    • Powerball have 5 balls are drawn from a total of 69 + 1 Power Ball are drawn from a total of 26.
  • European Lottery:
    • The Euro Millions is a transnational lottery participated by 9 European countries.
    • Euro Millions have 5 balls are drawn from a total of 50 + 2 Lucky Stars are drawn from a total of 12.
    • Results of the Euro Million Lucky Stars will be based on alphanumeric order as shown in the official website.
Bet Types

Chinese Zodiac – Classification

  • Predict the result from the group type of zodiac animals.

Chinese Zodiac – Trines

  • Predict the result from the grouping of zodiac animals

Pick The Chinese Zodiac

  • Predict the result of the specified number represented by the zodiac drawn.

Specified Ball and Adjacent Numbers

  • Predict the result of the specific number drawn or the number beside it. For example: Bonus Ball result is 5, if you buy any 4, 5 or 6, you win the bet.
  • The last number is adjacent to the first number and vice versa. For example, in Hong Kong Mark Six; Bonus Ball result is 1, if you bet on 49, 1 or 2, you win the bet.

Range Betting

  • Predict the result of the specified ball drawn in relation to the specified range.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the result of the specified ball drawn will be odd or even.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the result of the specified ball drawn will be over or under the indicated number.

Pick the Bonus Ball

  • Predict the result of the specified ball drawn.

Pick the First / Second / Third Number

  • Predict the result of the specified number drawn.
