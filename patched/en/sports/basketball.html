General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • If a game is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets are considered void. If a game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets are considered void, unless otherwise stated in the individual bet type rules. All 1st half bets will still be valid.
  • For Quarter / Half Betting, the period must be completed for bets to be valid, unless explicitly stated below or in the individual Bet Type rules.
  • NCAA Venue Rule: Please note, that the "Home" and "Away" venue indicated on the website is for reference only. Bets will stand regardless of change of venue, be it to the "Home" team's venue, the "Away" team's venue or to a "Neutral" venue.
  • If the game starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the game commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the game commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.
  • Overtime is included for any Game, 2nd Half betting or 2-leg tied match, unless otherwise stated. 4th Quarter betting does not include overtime
  • For 3 x 3 Basketball, settlement will be based on official competition rules, especially for Street ball and Big 3 match versions of the sport.
Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will win the game. This market will contain the two teams.
  • Markets offered may include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.
  • If the game is suspended or cancelled during the 2nd half, all 1st half bets will still be valid.
  • If the game is suspended or cancelled during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets will be considered void.
  • Markets offered may include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.

In-Play Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Markets offered may include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.

Total Points: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • Markets offered may include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets will be void unless they have been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 1st half bets will still be valid.
  • If the game is abandoned during the 2nd half, all 2nd half bets will be void unless they have been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.
  • If the game is abandoned, all period bets will be void unless one of the two occurs:
    • The period was already completed before the game was abandoned.
    • The market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result.

Total Points: Over / Under (In Play)

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line. Settlement is based on the final score line and the total line is applied to a 0-0 score line.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Team Points - Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points recorded by the stated team will be over or under the indicated line.
  • Should the game be abandoned all bets will be considered void unless the market has been already settled or unconditionally determined as any further points will have no effect on the market result.
  • All settlements will be based on post-match statistics provided by the relevant sporting body.

Total Points: Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be odd or even.
  • Markets offered may include Full Game, Match Halves or Quarter Betting.

Team to Score First

  • Predict which team will score first in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and a team has already scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If neither team has scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 4 quarters of normal play and overtime, then bets are considered void.

Team to Score Last

  • Predict which team will score last in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 4 quarters of normal play and overtime, then bets are considered void.

Team with the Highest Scoring Quarter

  • Predict which team will score the most in a single quarter.
  • Overtime does not count.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 4 quarters of normal play, then bets are considered void.

First Team to Score 20 Points in Each Quarter

  • Predict which team will be the first to score 20 points in each quarter.
  • Overtime does not count.
  • If a game is abandoned, bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team scores 20 points in each quarter, then bets are considered void.
  • The number of points could vary, depending on the game. This will be clearly marked in the bet type.

Basketball Specials

  • Predict the number of points, steal ball, rebounds, assists, three points, etc.
  • Both players / teams must play in the game for bets to be considered valid.
  • If one or both players / teams do not take part in the game, all bets will be considered void.
  • Bets will be settled according to the NBA or specific governing body's result announced at the end of the game and any subsequent changes to the statistics are considered invalid for betting purposes.

Team Points - Last Digit

  • Predict the last digit of the final score for the home or away team.

Fantasy Basketball Game Rules

  • Fantasy Basketball bets involve selecting any 2 teams out of any 2 matches (not playing in the same match) and playing on the same day.
  • Fantasy match results will be based on the actual result of points scored; any handicap given for the basketball fantasy game will be taken into account for the final result of the fantasy match.
  • The two teams involved in Fantasy matches must play on the same day for the bets to be considered valid.
  • If a team's next fixture is played on a different day from that displayed in the company website, all Fantasy match-bets involving this team will be void.
  • The venues of these games are not considered in these fantasy game match ups.
  • Here is an example of a Fantasy Game:
    • Boston Celtic 101 - 98 Chicago Bulls, LA Lakers 118 - 101 Orlando Magic
    • Fantasy Game 1: Boston Celtic vs LA Lakers
    • Boston Celtic 101 - LA Lakers 118
    • The result will be based on the actual result of the respective matches.
  • A team's next fixture must be completed and have its result upheld by the competition's official governing body (e.g. NBA) in order for Fantasy match-bets involving this team to stand. If a team's next fixture is not completed or its result is overturned by the competition's official governing body, bets on that team will not stand and shall be considered void.
  • All fantasy matches will follow existing Basketball rules.

Winning Margin

  • Predict the number of points that separate the wining teams from the losing team for the period defined.
  • Markets offered may include Full Time, Match Halves or Quarters Betting.
E-BASKETBALL
  • These matches may be offered in virtual simulation or player format.
  • Settlement will be based on the official score at the end of the match duration stated within the Competition Name (e.g. 4x4, 4x5 Mins Play)
  • Should no duration be stated, settlement will be based on the official score of the match under that specific competition.
  • Should a virtual simulation or player v player match fail to finish due to technical or any other reason, all wagers will be considered void.
