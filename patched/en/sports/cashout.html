CASH OUT RULES

Last Update: 02/09/2019

General Rules

  • Cash Out is only available on selected sports and market types.
  • The availability of Cash Out may be withdrawn or adjusted at any time. Customers should never assume Cash Out will be available.
  • Cash Out values are non-negotiable. The company will not enter into discussion about the reason for any Cash Out value offered.

  • For partial Cash Out, the minimum unit stake (per market type) must remain on either side of the partial cash out range.

    Example 1 – Minimum Unit Stake = 1.00 / Customer Stake = 100.00. Bets can be partially cashed out in the range of 1.00 - 99.00.
    Example 2 - Minimum Unit Stake = 1.00 / Customer Stake = 5.00. Bets can be partially cashed out in the range of 1.00 - 4.00.
    Example 3 – Minimum Unit Stake = 1.00 / Customer Stake = 1.00. Bets cannot be partially cashed out. However, they can still be fully cashed out.

  • The Cash Out value displayed for any bet is the amount returned to your account should the Cash Out be successful. This includes the stake value related to the cashed-out amount.

    Example 1 – If Cash Out is showing 1,500.00 and the related stake was 1,000.00, the Win / Loss if confirmed would be + 500.00 (Win)
    Example 2 – If Cash Out is showing 800.00 and the related stake was 1,000.00, the Win / Loss if confirmed would be - 200.00 (Loss)

Settlement Rules

  • Should an event be cancelled or postponed, the company reserves the right to cancel and reverse any previously cashed out bet, unless the market has been unconditionally determined.
  • Should an event be suspended and fail to resume within 36hours, the company reserves the right to cancel and reverse any previously cashed out bet, unless the market has been unconditionally determined.
  • Should a cashed out wager be subject to cancellation, the company reserves the right to cancel that wager and reverse the cash out.


Settlement Examples

  • Single Bets – Fully Cashed Out

    Should a single bet be fully cashed out for an event or wager which is later cancelled, the cashed out amount will be returned to the company and the original stake amount returned to the customer.

    However, should the cashed out bet have been for a specific period (e.g. Football 1st Half or Basketball 1st Quarter) and the event was cancelled after these periods had been completed, the Cash Out will be valid.

  • Single Bet – Partially Cashed Out

    Should a single bet be partially cashed out for an event or wager which is later cancelled, the cashed out amount will be returned to the company and the partially cashed out stake amount returned to the customer.

    The remaining stake (not included in the initial partial Cash Out) will also be void and returned to the customer’s account.

    However, should the cashed out bet have been for a specific period (e.g. Football 1st Half or Basketball 1st Quarter) and the event was cancelled after these periods had been completed, the Cash Out and remaining stake will be valid.

  • Multiples Bets – when a selection event is cancelled.

    Should one or more selection from a multiple bet be cancelled, any previous cash out will be void, unless the market had been unconditionally determined.

    For straight accumulators (X1), any remaining selections will be settled at the original stake. For example, a 5-fold (of selections A, B, C, D and E) where B is voided, will be settled as a 4-fold for the combined odds of A, C, D and E.

    Should any of the remaining selections be available for betting, then the possibility to Cash Out may once again appear. However, should all the remaining selections be settled, then the bet will be settled accordingly.
