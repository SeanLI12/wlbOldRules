EACH WAY (E/W) Rules

Last Update: 15/12/2017

 

Each Way consists of 2 separate bets (of equal stake amount) on a single selection; a Win bet and a Place bet.

  1. If the selection wins, both stakes will be returned along with the calculated winnings.
  2. If the selection loses, a limited return may still be applied should the allowed Place terms be met.

Once confirmed, an Each Way bet will display in 2 parts: Win and Place.

Each available market has its own Place terms (Finishing Position and Odds) and these define the amount that may be won.

Place terms are usually represented as, for example, 1/4 - Top 3. This means one quarter of the original odds will be returned (on half the total stake) should the selection finish in the top three places of an event. Place terms vary and could be 1/5 - Top 5 or 1/5 - Top 7 etc.

Possible outcomes of an Each Way bet are:

  1. Win and Place parts both 'Win'.
  2. Win part 'Loses' & Place part 'Wins'.
  3. Win and Place parts both 'Lose'.
Full examples of each may be found below General Rules.

General Rules

  • Each Way bets can only be placed on specific Outright Markets. The General Rules of each Sport will apply to the settlement of these bets.
  • Each Way terms (places and odds) will be subject to change as an event progresses, e.g. a Golf Tournament. The terms defined (in your statement) at the time the bet was struck will be honored, so long as the market is each way enabled.
  • Each Way bets may be voided, should the number of starting participants prevent the formation of an Each Way market. For example, 5 scheduled participants are reduced to 3 prior to the event starting.
  • Each Way Multiples (e.g. Doubles, Trebles, 4-Folds etc.) can only be placed when all selections within the betslip are Each Way enabled.
  • Settlement of Each Way Multiples is based on Win-to-Win and Place-to-Place (not a combination of both). For example, if 2 selections (E/W Double) resulted in 1 Win and 1 Place, the Win-to-Win stake will be lost, while the Place-to-Place stake will win.
  • Settlement of Each Way Win and Place (or Place only) bets are subject to Dead Heat Rules.

Examples:

  1. Selection Wins (Finished 1st)

    Outright Markets - English Premier League (Winner)

    Selection: Arsenal @ 13.0  |  Stake: 100.00  |  E/W Terms - 1/4 Odds, Top 3

    Total Stake = 200.00 (inc. Each Way)

    Result: Arsenal wins the league.

    Win → 13.0 (Win odds) X 100 (Win stake) = 1,200.00

    Place → 4.0 (Place odds) X 100 (Place stake) = 300.00

    Win / Loss is 1,200 + 300 = + 1,500.00

  2. Selection Placed (Finished in Top 3)

    Outright Markets - English Premier League (Winner)

    Selection: Arsenal @ 13.0  |  Stake: 100.00  |  E/W Terms - 1/4 Odds, Top 3

    Total Stake = 200.00 (inc. Each Way)

    Result: Arsenal finished third.

    Win → Arsenal did not win the league; therefore this is a losing bet = -100.00

    Place → 4.0 (Place odds) X 100 (Place stake) = 300.00

    Win / Loss is -100 + 300 = + 200.00

  3. Selection Loses (Finished outside of Top 3)

    Outright Markets - English Premier League (Winner)

    Selection: Arsenal @ 13.0  |  Stake: 100.00  |  E/W Terms - 1/4 Odds, Top 3

    Total Stake = 200.00 (inc. Each Way)

    Result: Arsenal finished fourth.

    Win → Arsenal did not win the league; therefore this is a losing bet = -100.00

    Place → Arsenal did not finish in the Top 3; therefore this is a losing bet = -100.00

    Win / Loss is -100 + -100 = - 200.00
