FUTSAL

Last Update: 21/09/2016

General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • All markets are settled after the full 40 minutes is played.
  • All Futsal bets are settled based on the result at the end of 2 Halves (20 minutes each), unless otherwise stated.
  • If the game starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the game commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the game commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.
Bet Types

1 X 2

  • Predict who will win the game. This market will contain the two teams and the draw as betting selections.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Handicap (In-Play)

  • Predict who will win the match / period with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Settlement is based on the score line, from when the bet was placed to the end of the match / period - i.e. the final score minus the current score of the match.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Over / Under (In-Play)

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • Settlement is based on the final score line and the total line is applied to a 0-0 score line.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be odd or even.

Team to Score First

  • Predict which team will score first in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and a team has already been scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If neither team has scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 2 halves of normal play, then bets are considered void.

Team to Score Last

  • Predict which team will score last in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void..
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 2 halves of normal play, then bets are considered void.
