General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will stand so long as the venue is not changed to an opponent’s ground. International venues may be changed, so long as the new venue is within the same country.
  • All batsman markets will be settled on runs scored by the named batsman. Extra’s recorded while a batsmen is on strike will not be attributed their total number of runs in that Innings.
Test Matches & County Championship Matches
  • Bets will stand based on the official result provided at least one ball has been bowled. In the event of a tie, all bets on Winner will be void and bets on the draw in 1 x 2 will be settled as losing bets.

    Tie vs. Draw

    The result of a match is ‘Tie’ when the scores are equal at the conclusion of play after both teams completed their innings, or, in Limited Overs Cricket, the set number of overs has been played, or play is terminally stopped by weather or bad light.

    ‘Draw’ is the inconclusive result that occurs when a team does not complete its innings at the scheduled end of play. It only applies to First Class Cricket.

  • If a match is delayed due to rain (or any other reason) all bets will be settled based on the official result.
  • In Test matches, “Batsman Runs”, “Innings Runs” & “Player Match Bets” stand regardless of the length of the innings.
  • If a match is abandoned due to external interference, bets will be considered void.
Limited Overs Matches
  • In one-day matches, if the match is cut short or abandoned due to weather or external interference, all bets will be settled according to the official competition rules. This includes matches affected by the Duckworth-Lewis Method (D/L Method) or the Jayadevan system (JVD). If the final result is determined by a bowl out or coin toss, all bets will be considered void.
  • If the match ends in a tie and the official result or the competition rules do not determine a winner, then all bets on Winner will be void. In competitions where a bowl out or a super over determines a winner, all bets will be considered void.
  • Where the Duckworth Lewis Method (D/L method) is used, all bets placed on "Highest 10 over Total", "Highest Opening Partnership", “Batsmen Match Bet” and “Bowler Match Bet” will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined before the D/L method was implemented. Batsmen/Bowler Match Bets offered after D/L method will be settled based on the new overs.
  • Should a new toss take place on a scheduled reserve day for One Day International, all bets during the last 45 minutes to the start at the original scheduled first day match will be considered void. This rule applies to all markets, except those have been unconditionally determined, such as "Win The Toss"

Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict which team will win the match. This market will contain 2 teams.
  • In First Class Cricket, if the result of the match is a draw, all bets will be void.
  • If there is no official result, all bets will be void.

1 x 2

  • Predict which team will be the match winner or whether the match will end as a draw.
  • In Limited Over, the draw (x) will be settled as tie.
  • If there is no official result, all bets will be void.

Draw Result

  • Predict whether the match will end as a draw.
  • If the result of the match is a draw, then all ‘yes’ selections will be settled as winning bets.

To Win the Toss

  • Predict which team will win the coin toss prior to the start of the match.
  • If a result was not determined all bets will be void.

Top Team Batsman

  • Predict who will be the top Batsman in a specific match based on the number of runs scored.
  • Bets placed on any player not in the starting 11 or nominated as the designated substitute 12th man will then be considered void.
  • In a 2-innings match (First Class Cricket), this market applies to the first innings only, unless otherwise stated.
  • Bets placed on players who are selected but do not bat or do not field will be settled as losing bets.
  • Dead-Heat rule applies if no winner has been determined.

Top Team Bowler

  • Predict who will be the top Bowler in a specific match based on the number of wickets taken.
  • Bets placed on any player not in the starting 11 or nominated as the designated substitute 12th man will then be considered void.
  • In a 2-innings match (First Class Cricket), this market applies to the first innings only, unless otherwise stated.
  • Bets placed on players who are selected but do not bat or do not field will be settled as losing bets.
  • In the event of players taking the same number of wickets, the winner will be determined as the bowler that has conceded the least number of runs, irrespective of overs bowled by each player.
  • Dead-Heat rule applies if no winner has been determined.

Most Match Fours

  • Predict which team will record the most fours in the match.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Most Match Sixes

  • Predict which team will record the most sixes in the match.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Most Match Boundaries

  • Predict which team will record the most match boundaries (fours and sixes combined) in the match.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Highest Opening Partnership

  • Predict which team will record the highest opening partnership in the match.
  • Only the 1st batting Innings for each team will count towards this market.
  • The draw will also be a selection.

Runs before Fall of 1st Wicket/ Runs before Fall of X (next) Wicket

  • Predict the number of runs recorded before the batting team’s first (or next) player is dismissed.
  • One delivery must be bowled otherwise all bets on this market will be considered void.
  • If no dismissal is recorded the bet will be void unless already unconditionally determined.
  • Run totals for the batting team will include Target total or Declaration total.

1st Wicket Method

  • Predict the method by which the first batsman will be dismissed.
  • The selections for this market will be caught and other.
  • If no wickets are recorded by the bowling team, then any bet on this market will be considered void.

Next Man Out

  • Predict which of the two active batsmen will be dismissed next.
  • If one batsman retires, all bets will be considered void.

Runs Scored from 1st Ball

  • Predict the number of runs scored from 1st delivery bowled in the match.
  • If no ball is bowled in the match, then all bets on this market will be considered void.

Total Team Fours

  • Predict whether the total number of fours scored by the stated team will be over or under the value indicated.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Team Sixes

  • Predict whether the total number of sixes scored by the stated team will be over or under the value indicated.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Match Boundaries

  • Predict whether the total number of match boundaries (fours and sixes combined) scored by the stated team will be over or under the value indicated.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Team Runs 2-Way (or 3-Way)

  • Predict whether the total number of runs scored by the current batting team will be over or under the value indicated.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void..

Team to Score Most Runs in X Over

  • Predict which team will record the most number of runs from the stated over (X) of their respective Innings.
  • If either team fails to complete the stated over (X), then all bets on this market will be considered void, unless the result has been unconditionally determined.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.

1st Over Runs 2-Way

  • Predict whether the total number of runs recorded by the stated team (within the 1st over) will be over or under the value indicated.
  • If that team fails to complete the first over, then all bets on this market will be considered void, unless the result has been unconditionally determined.

Highest 1st Over

  • Predict which team will record the most number of runs from the 1st over of their respective 1st Innings.
  • If either team fails to complete a first over, then all bets on this market will be considered void, unless the result has been unconditionally determined.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.

Team All Out

  • Predict whether the active batting team will lose all 10 wickets before the 1st Innings is declared over.
  • If the match is abandoned before the end of the 1st Innings then all bets will be void.

Batsmen Match Bet

  • Predict which of the batsmen stated will record the most runs in that innings.
  • If one batsman retires, all bets will be considered void.
  • In the event of a tie, bets will be void.

Bowler Match Bet

  • Predict which of the bowlers stated will record the most wickets taken in that innings.
  • Each player must bowl at least one ball; otherwise any bets on this market will be void.
  • In the event of a tie, bets will be void.

X Over Runs 2-Way

  • Predict whether the total number of runs scored for the stated period, e.g. 5 overs, will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If the stated period is not fully completed, then all bets will be void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

X Over Runs 3-Way

  • Predict whether the total number of runs scored for the stated period, e.g. 5 overs, will be under, between or over the indicated range of values.
  • If the stated period is not fully completed, then all bets will be void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Total Runs 2-Way (Player)

  • Predict whether the stated player’s record runs will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.
  • If a batsman retires hurt and does not return, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.
  • If offered during a First Class Cricket match, then this proposition will only count for the Innings stated.

Total Runs 3-Way (Player)

  • Predict whether the stated player’s recorded runs, will be under, between or under the indicated range of values.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.
  • If a batsman retires hurt and does not return, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.
  • If offered during a First Class Cricket match, then this proposition will only count for the Innings stated.

To Score 50 / 100 / 150 / 200 Runs (Player)

  • Predict whether a player will record an innings total of 50 / 100 / 150 / 200 or more runs.
  • Limited Over Settlement – Bets will be void for rain or any delay, unless unconditionally determined.
  • First Class Settlement – Bets will be settled based on the officially declared final score, irrespective of rain or any other delay.
  • If offered during a First Class Cricket match, then this proposition will only count for the Innings stated.

Method of Dismissal (Next Wicket)

  • Predict the method by which the next batsman will be dismissed.
  • The types and numbers of selections available may depend on the form of Cricket match (Limited Overs or First Class) and the period of the match.
  • If no further batsman is dismissed then all bets on this market will be considered void.

Total Team Runs – Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total runs recorded by the active batting team will be odd or even.
  • One ball must be bowled in that Innings, otherwise bets on this market will be considered void.
  • Settlement will be based on the Innings score officially recorded by the governing body.

1st Over Runs – Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the runs recorded by the active batting team in the 1st over of that Innings will be odd or even.
  • One ball must be bowled in the 1st over, otherwise bets on this market will be considered void.

Most Match Wides

  • Predict which team will record the most wides in the match.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Match Wides

  • Predict whether the recorded number of match wides will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Match Run Outs

  • Predict whether the recorded number of match run-outs will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Most Match Stumpings

  • Predict which team will record the most stumpings in the match.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Match Stumpings

  • Predict whether the recorded number of match stumpings will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Most Match Ducks

  • Predict which team will record the most ducks in the match.
  • The draw will also be a selection option.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

Total Match Ducks

  • Predict whether the recorded number of match ducks will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If there is any reduction in the scheduled number of overs played, then bets on this market will be void.

First Innings Lead

  • Predict which team will lead after the First Innings has concluded.
  • Both teams must complete their First Innings for bets to stand (including declarations).

lnnings Runs 2-Way (or 3-Way)

  • Predict whether the selected participant’s recorded runs will be over or under the indicated value.
  • If there is any reduction due to rain or any other delay, then the following conditions will apply.

    Limited Overs
    All bets will be void, unless already determined.

    First Class Matches
    All bets will stand, regardless of rain or any other delay.

Session Runs 2-Way

  • Predict whether the total number of runs scored in the current session will be over or under the indicated value.
  • A minimum of 20 overs must be completed for bets to stand.
