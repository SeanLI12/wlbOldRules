GENERAL RULES

  • All bets will only be considered valid when a match or game is completed, unless explicitly stated below or in the individual bet types.
  • The result of an event or game will be determined at the time of conclusion. For betting purposes, overturned decisions will not be recognized after a match or game has concluded.
  • In the event of a disqualification or walkover, all bets will be considered void unless explicitly stated in the individual bet type rules.
  • If a match or game were to be postponed or suspended and not resume within 36 hours from the official start time, all completed games will be settled based on the official results. All other bets will be void.
  • In the event of a “Re-make” or “Re-creation” before the match has concluded, bets will be settled based on the official result.
  • If a match begins before the official scheduled time, all the bets placed after the match has started will be considered void, except for In-Play bets.
  • In-Play bets are settled based on the final score after the handicap is applied to a 0-0 score line. The live score at the time of bet placement is not factored into the bet.
  • If the number of games/rounds played is different from what is stated in the betting market, all bets will be considered void.
  • If a team uses a known alias to compete & play with at least one official player, all bets will stand.
  • If a match is played with fewer players on either or both teams, all bets will stand.

BET TYPES

Winner

  • Predict who will win the match or specified game. Any form of extra time or overtime will count.
  • Bets will stand once the specified match or game has been completed.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win with the indicated Handicap applied. Any form of extra time or overtime will count.
  • The Handicap in an Esports match can apply to Games / Rounds/ Kills or other counting measures specified.
  • Bets will stand once the specified match or game has been completed.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of Maps / Rounds/ Kills or other counting measures specified will be over or under the indicated line. Any form of extra time or overtime will count for settlement purposes.
  • Bets will stand once the specified match or game has been completed.

Proposition

  • Examples of Propositions include, but are not limited to

    • First to Reach 5/10 Kills
    • First Turret/Tower
    • First Baron
    • First Roshan
    • Duration – Over/Under
    • R1/16 Pistol Round
    • R1/16 Total Kills 7.5
  • In the event of a disqualification or walkover, all bets will stand once the event has occurred or has been unconditionally determined.
  • In the event of a “re-make” or “re-creation” before the game has concluded, all bets will be settled based on the official result.

STREAM BASED BETTING RULES

General Rules

  • These markets will be settled using data recorded in Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
  • Data recorded for each streamer is based on their registered in-game name, not their streamer ID.
  • For betting purposes, the game number sequence will restart (from one) after 23:59 UTC.
  • The game number includes all games played by the streamer that day, irrespective of the game mode played.
  • Only predefined game modes per eSport will be counted.
  • Specific rules for each eSport will be followed.

PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG)

  • Single, Duo and Squad games will be counted.
  • Recorded kills will only include those singularly achieved by the streamer. It will not include kills recorded as part of duo or squad games.
  • For duo or squad games, the final match standing will be determined by team performance.

League of Legends (LOL)

  • Only Ranked Solo/Duo Queue games will be counted.
  • The game will be voided if:
    • Streamer quits before the match ends.
    • Game duration is less than 5 minutes.
    • Streamer invites a friend on Ranked Solo/Duo games
  • Streamer Kills, Deaths, and Assists will only include those singularly achieved by the streamer.
  • For KDA markets, Streamer KDA is defined as (Kills + Assists) / Deaths. If the streamer did not die at any point in the game, his KDA is computed as Kills + Assists
