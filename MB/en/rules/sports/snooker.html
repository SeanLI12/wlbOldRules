SNOOKER / POOL

Last Update: 29/03/2022

General Rules
  • All bets will be considered valid only when the match is finished, unless otherwise stated.
  • A Frame or Rack is considered valid if it is not played but awarded by WPBSA (World Professional Billiards and Snooker Association) to a player.
  • If a match starts before schedule, only the transactions before the first point starts will be considered valid. The transactions after the first point of the match will be considered invalid. (Except in-play bet types).
  • 'Play all X' format implies that all indicated number of frames/racks will be played in a match. In 'Play All 7', this may result to different possible outcomes with a total of 7 frames/racks. (e.g.: 6-1, 3-4, 2-5, etc.)
Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will win the match.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the match with the indicated frame / rack handicap applied.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of frames / racks in a match will be over or under the indicated total line.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of frames / racks played in a match will be odd or even.

Frame Betting

  • Predict which player will win or will be awarded as the winner by WPBSA on specified frame during a specified match.

‘Play All’ Matches

  • This match format is based on all frames being played. For example 'Play All 8' means the final result could be 7-1 or 4-4 etc.
