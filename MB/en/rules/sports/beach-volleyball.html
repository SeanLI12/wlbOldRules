BEACH VOLLEYBALL

Last Update: 20/09/2016

General Rules
  • All bets will be considered valid only when the match is finished, unless otherwise stated.
  • If any of the named players in a match changes before the match starts, the company reserved the right to void all bets.
  • Bets will be settled based on the team that wins the first 2 sets out of the total 3 sets played.
  • If the scheduled duration of a match is reduced or if there is an increase in the number of points to win, all bets will be considered void.
  • If a match starts before schedule, only the transactions before the game starts will be considered valid. The transactions after the match starts will be considered invalid. (Except in-play bet types).

Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will win the match.

Set Handicap

  • Predict who will win the match with the indicated set handicap applied.

Point Handicap

  • Predict who will win more points in a specified period (e.g. Match, 1st Set, 2nd Set) after applying the indicated handicap.

Total Points: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points in a specified period (e.g. Match, 1st Set, 2nd Set) will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of points in a specified period (e.g. Match, 1st Set, 2nd Set) will be odd or even.
