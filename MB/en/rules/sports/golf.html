General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • Dead heat rules apply for settlement purposes.
  • If a tournament ends officially before the stipulated time and the trophy is awarded, all bets on the tournament are considered valid. Bets accepted after the play for the day has ended, are considered void, unless there is further play which contributes to the results of the tournament (playoffs not included).
  • If a tournament is officially abandoned, all bets on the tournament are void, except markets that have already been decided.
Bet Types

Outright

  • Predict who will win the tournament. Bets are settled based on which player is awarded the trophy.
  • A player is deemed to have started, once they have teed off. If a player later withdraws, retires or is disqualified, then all bets will be valid.
  • "Any Other Player Not Listed" refers to all golfers not named in the outright market.
  • Outright markets are settled based on the tournament winner and any play-offs are taken into consideration in the final result.

Head to Head / 2 Balls / 3 Balls (18 Holes)

  • Predict who will complete the round in the lowest score.
  • Players are paired for the purpose of the bet. They may or may not be playing together in the actual competition.
  • The winner will be the player with the lowest score over 18 holes.
  • If a player fails to start the round, all the bets will be considered void.
  • Both players must complete all 18 holes for Round Match Bets. If one of the players finishes early either by agreement of the players or through injury, all bets are considered void.
  • For 2 Balls, the tie will be a betting selection.
  • For 3 Balls, the tie will not be a betting selection. Instead, dead heat rules apply in the case of a tie.

Head to Head / 2 Balls / 3 Balls (72 Holes / Tournament Match Bet)

  • Predict who will complete the tournament in the lowest score.
  • Players are paired for the purpose of the bet. They may or may not be playing together in the actual competition.
  • The winner will be the player with the lowest score over 72 holes, including any playoff holes.
  • If a player fails to start the round, all the bets will be considered void.
  • Both players must complete all 72 holes for Tournament Match Bets. If one of the players finishes early either by agreement of the players or through injury, all bets are considered void.
  • For 2 Balls, the tie will be a betting selection.
  • For 3 Balls, the tie will not be a betting selection. Instead, dead heat rules apply in the case of a tie.

Next Hole Score - Single Player Market

  • Predict the Next Hole Score to be recorded by a given or selected player (Birdie or better / Par / Bogey or worse)
  • If a player fails to start the respective hole, then bets will be considered void.
  • Once the player has teed off for the stated hole, bets will be valid. If a player fails to finish the hole (injury withdrawal etc) then 'over-par' score will be awarded.
  • Settlement will be based upon the official post-hole score as recorded by the relevant governing body (European Tour / PGA)

Handicap

  • Predict who will complete the round / tournament in the lowest score. A virtual start is given to one player. If both players get the same scores after taking the handicap into consideration, it will be considered a tie. All stakes will then be returned.

Over / Under (Score)

  • Predict whether the final score of both players will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • In the case of abandonment, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Top 5 / 10 Finish

  • Predict if the player will finish in the top 5 / 10 in the specific tournament.
  • Dead-Heat rules apply.
  • For betting purposes, bets will be settled at the end of the scheduled tournament.

Winning Margin

  • Predict the difference in strokes between the winning player and the runner-up at the end of the tournament.
  • For betting purposes, bets will be settled at the end of the scheduled tournament.
  • In the event of a playoff, results from the playoff will not be counted towards the final result.
