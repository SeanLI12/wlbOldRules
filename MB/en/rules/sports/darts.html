DARTS

Last Update: 20/09/2016

General Rules
  • All bets will be considered valid only when the match is finished, unless otherwise stated.
  • If a player fails to compete in a match, all bets on that player will be considered void.
  • If a player retires or is disqualified during a match before its completion, all bets for the match will be considered void, unless otherwise stated.
  • If a match starts before schedule, only the transactions before the game starts will be considered valid. The transactions after the match starts will be considered invalid. (Except in-play bet types).
Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will be the winner of the specified match.
  • If a match fails to finish, the player declared the winner by the relevant governing body will be used for settlement.
  • This market may be offered as 2-Way or 3-Way depending on the event.
  • If a 2-Way market results in a draw, then all bets will be void. The option of draw will be included in 3-Way selections.

Winner (Set)

  • Predict who will be the winner of a specified set stated in the market name.
  • If the specific set fails to finish, then all bets on this market will be considered void.
  • If the specific set does finish, but the match does not, then all bets on this market will stand.

Winner (Leg)

  • Predict who will be the winner of the specific leg stated in the market name.
  • The leg may be specific to a 'Legs' match or as leg of a 'Sets' match.
  • If the specific leg fails to finish, then all bets on this market will be considered void.
  • If the specific set does finish, but the match does not, then all bets on this market will stand.

Match Correct Score

  • Predict the correct score at the end of the stated match.
  • If the match is based on 'Legs', then the overall number of legs recorded by each player will apply.
  • If the match is based on 'Sets', then the overall number of sets recorded by each player will apply.
  • If the match is not completed in full, then all bets will be void.

Handicap

  • Predict the outcome of the match with the indicated handicap applied.
  • If the match is based on 'Legs', then the handicap offered will be based on the final leg score.
  • If the match is based on 'Sets', then the handicap offered will be based on the final set score.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Total Legs: Over / Under

  • Predict whether to the total number of match legs will be over or under the stated value.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Total Legs - X Set

  • Predict the exact number of legs played within the specific set stated.
  • If the specific set does not finish, then all bets will be void.
  • If the specific set does finish, but the match does not, then all bets on this market will stand.

Total Sets: Over / Under

  • Predict whether to the total number of match sets will be over or under the stated value.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Race to 3 Legs

  • Predict who will be the first player to record 3 winnings legs.
  • If either person fails to record 3 wining legs in the match, then all bets will be void.

Score after 4 Legs

  • Predict the correct score for a specific player after the first 4 legs of the match have been played.
  • If the first 4 legs of the match are not completed, then all bets will be void.

To Lead after 4 Legs

  • Predict the player to be leading, after the first 4 legs of the match have been played.
  • Draw will also be available for selection.
  • If the first 4 legs of the match are not completed, then all bets will be void.

Correct Score - X Set

  • Predict the correct leg score at the end of the specific set stated.
  • If the specific set does not finish, then all bets will be void.
  • If the specific set does finish, but the match does not, then all bets on this market will stand.

180 Scored

  • Predict whether a 180 will be scored in the specific period stated.
  • The market may be based on a specific leg of a 'Legs' match or of a specific leg of a 'Sets' match.
  • If a player's name is not stated in the market name, the result will be based on either player recording a 180.
  • If the players name is included in the market name, the result will be based on that player’s total 180’s in the match.
  • If the specific leg fails to finish, then all bets on that market will be considered void.

Match 180's - Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of 180's recorded in the match will be over or under the value stated.
  • If a player's name is not stated in the market name, the result will be based on total 180's recorded by both players.
  • If the players name is included in the market name, the result will be based on that player’s total 180’s in the match.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Match 180's - Player Handicap

  • Predict the outcome of match 180's with the indicated handicap applied to each player.
  • Settlement will be based on the total number of 180's recorded by each player at the end of the match.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Most 180's Scored

  • Predict which player will record the higher number of 180's in the match.
  • The result of draw may also be included as a selection.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Match Checkout

  • Predict which player will record the winning checkout in the final leg of the match.
  • If the match fails to finish, then all bets will be considered void.

Checkout Total: Over / Under X

  • Predict if the checkout total for a specific leg of a match is over or under the value indicated.
  • If the match is based on 'Legs', then the market offered will be based on a specific leg stated.
  • If the match is based on 'Sets', then the market offered will be based on a specific leg within a set.
  • If the specific leg fails to finish, then all bets will be considered void.

Checkout Total: Over / Under (X-X)

  • Predict if the checkout total for a specific leg of a match falls between the range stated (inclusive of both numbers) or whether the checkout total will be above or below that range.
  • If the match is based on 'Legs', then the market offered will be based on a specific leg stated.
  • If the match is based on 'Sets', then the market offered will be based on a specific leg within a set.
  • If the specific leg fails to finish, then all bets will be considered void.

Highest Match Checkout: Over / Under X

  • Predict if the highest match checkout will be over or under the value stated. The highest possible checkout being 170.
  • If a player's name is not stated in the market name, the result will be based on checkouts recorded by both players.
  • If the players name is included in the market name, the result will be based on the specific player’s highest checkout.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Highest Match Checkout: Over / Under (X-X)

  • Predict if the highest match checkout falls between the range stated (inclusive of both numbers) or whether the highest match checkout will be above or below that range. The highest possible checkout being 170.
  • If a player's name is not stated in the market name, the result will be based on checkouts recorded by both players.
  • If the players name is included in the market name, the result will be based on the specific player’s highest checkout.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.

Checkout Colour

  • Predict the colour of the checkout dart (i.e. last dart) for the specific period of the match stated.
  • If the match is based on 'Legs', then the market offered will be based on a specific leg stated.
  • If the match is based on 'Sets', then the market offered will be based on a specific leg within a set.
  • The selections offered will always be 'Red' or 'Green'
  • If the specific leg fails to finish, then all bets will be considered void.

Highest Match Checkout

  • Predict which player will record the highest checkout value for any leg of the match. The maximum possible checkout being 170.
  • If the match does not finish, then all bets will be void unless the market has been unconditionally determined.
