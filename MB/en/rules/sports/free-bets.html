Free Bet Rules

Last Update: 16/05/2022

 

General Rules

  • Free Bets may only be used in accordance with the conditions specified within the Free Bet details. The details of how a Free Bet can be used is available in the information icon of each Free Bet token in the Free Bet Lobby.
  • Free Bets are subject to Sportsbook General Rules, as well as any specific sports rules which may supersede them.
  • Free Bets must be used before their expiry date. Once a Free Bet expires, it can no longer be used.
  • Each Free Bet must be used in its entirety in a single transaction. This includes Each Way bets where the value of the Free Bet will be split in two.
  • Free Bets will not be included in any bet returns and are not available for withdrawal.
  • Where Free Bets cannot be used due to a specific event or market not being available, the company will not be responsible or reissue another Free Bet as compensation. 
  • Any bet placed with a Free Bet will not be available for Cash Out.
  • Free Bets may be subject to specific sport, market and odd restrictions and cannot be used on certain promotional markets or Price Boosts. 

Settlement Rules

  • All Free Bets placed will be settled in accordance with regular bet settlement rules. The status of each bet can be found within ‘My Bets’ and ‘Statement’.
  • Free Bets will only return the ‘Winnings’ associated with it. The value of the Free Bet will not be returned once the bet has settled.
  • Free Bets used on markets where the settlement outcome is ‘Push’ will not be reissued another Free Bet of the same value.
  • Free Bets used on markets where the settlement outcome is ‘Half Push / Half Lose’ will not be reissued another Free Bet for half the original value.

Settlement Examples

  • A Free Bet stake of 100.00 is used on a selection of Over 3.5 Goals with odds of 2.50 (Euro Odds)
    Should the final score be 4-0 (Settlement Status = Win), then 150.00 will be credited to the member’s account.
  • A Free Bet stake of 100.00 is used on a selection of Handicap +0.25 with odds of 1.90 (Euro Odds)
    Should the final score be 0-0 (Settlement Status = Half Push / Half Win), then 45.00 will be credited to the member’s account.
  • A Free Bet stake of 100.00 is used on a selection of Handicap -0.25 with odds of 1.90 (Euro Odds)
    Should the final score be 0-0 (Settlement Status = Half Push / Half Lose), then no funds or Free Bet token (related to this wager) will be credited to the member’s account.
  • A Free Bet stake of 100.00 is used on a selection of Handicap -1 with odds of 1.90 (Euro Odds)
    Should the final score be 1-0 (Settlement Status = Push), then no funds or Free Bet token (related to this wager) will be credited to the member’s account.
