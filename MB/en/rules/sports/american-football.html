AMERICAN FOOTBALL

Last Update: 15/12/2017

General Rules
  • The final result is the score after the four quarters, including overtime.
  • For Quarter / Half Betting, the period must be completed for bets to be valid.
  • 4th Quarter betting does not include overtime.
  • NCAA Venue Rule: Please note, that the "Home" and "Away" venue indicated on the website is for reference only. Bets will stand regardless of change of venue, be it to the "Home" team's venue, the "Away" team's venue or to a "Neutral" venue.
  • Overtime counts unless otherwise stated in the bet type rules below.
  • If the game starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the game commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the game commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.

Bet Types

Winner

  • Predict who will win the game. This market will contain the two teams.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Handicap (In-Play)

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Settlement is based on the final score line after the handicap is applied to a 0-0 score line. The current score, at the time of bet placement, is not factored into the bet.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line.

Over / Under (In-Play)

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • Settlement is based on the final score and the total line is applied to a 0-0 score line.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be odd or even. Overtime counts.

Half Time / Full Time

  • Predict the winning selection at both Half Time and Full Time after the four quarters in the game. Overtime counts.
  • This market is also known as "Double Result".

Winning Margin

  • This refers to the point difference between the 2 teams at the end of the 4 quarters. Overtime counts
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, all bets are void.

Team to Score First

  • Predict which team will score first in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and a team has already scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If neither team has scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 4 quarters of normal play and overtime, then bets are considered void.

Team to Score Last

  • Predict which team will score last in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 4 quarters of normal play and overtime, then bets are considered void.

Team with the Highest Scoring Quarter

  • Predict which team will score the most in a single quarter.
  • Overtime does not count.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled 4 quarters of normal play, then bets are considered void.

First Team to Score 10 Points in Each Quarter

  • Predict which team will be the first to score 10 points in each quarter.
  • Overtime does not count.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scores 10 points in each quarter, then bets are considered void.
  • The number of points could vary, depending on the game. This will be clearly marked in the bet type.
