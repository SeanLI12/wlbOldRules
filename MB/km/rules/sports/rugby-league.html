Rugby General Rules:

If one or more players do not take part in the match, all bets on that player(s) will be considered void and all wagers placed will be refunded to their original accounts. Accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.

Team to score first/ last

  • Once a team has scored the first try, all wagers on Team to Score First Try will stand.
  • Should a match be suspended and not resumed within 12 hrs form the original scheduled time, all wagers on last team to score will be considered void and all wagers placed will be refunded. Accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • If the match is abandoned at any time and a team has already scored first prior to the abandonment, then all bets for Team to Score First will stand.
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • If the match is abandoned at any time and no team has scored prior to the abandonment, then all bets on Team to Score First will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.

If a match stops for whatever reason before the end of the scheduled "80 minutes" play, all bets will be considered invalid.

If a match is postponed and rescheduled to play within the following 12 hours of the original schedule start time, then all bets will stand. If the match is postponed for more than 12 hours, all bets will be considered void.

If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
For international matches, as long as change in venue is within same country, all bets are considered valid.

For 1st half betting, bets are settled based on the result derived from the 1st haft of the match.
For 2nd half betting, bets are settled based on the result derived from the 2nd half of the match including any extra time.

If a match was aborted or cancelled during the 1st half, all 1st half bets considered void.
If match is suspended or cancelled during the 2nd half or during extra time, all 1st half bets will still be valid.

If match is suspended or cancelled during the 2nd half or during extra time, all 2nd half bets will be considered void.

TYPES OF WAGERS

IN-PLAY

  • To win: Over and Under In-Play wagers win/loss is determined by the SUM of the game.
  • If the game is postponed or suspended for any reasons and resumed within 12 hrs from the official kick off time, all rugby transactions will stand.
  • If the game is postponed or suspended for any reasons and resumed after 12 hrs from the official kick off time, all Rugby transactions will be voided and refunded.

Half Time / Full Time

  • Refers to betting on the Half and Full Time results of a designated match. For betting purposes, own goals are counted towards the final result. If a match venue is changed to away or neutral ground, all bets placed will be considered void and stakes will be refunded. If a match is abandoned at any time, all bets on Half Time/ Full Time will be considered void and bets will be refunded to their original accounts. Accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.

Top Try Scorer

  • This refers to betting on the player who will score the most tries in a regular season. Results will be settled on the outcome of a regular season only. Play-Offs do not count.

To Finish Bottom

  • This refers to betting on the team which will finish at the bottom of the league table at the end of the regular season, regardless of whether they are relegated or not.

1st Scoring Play

  • First scoring play try includes penalty tries. For all scoring play markets, any conversions made do not count. In the event that there is no score at the end of the match, all bets on 1st scoring play will be considered void and bets will be refunded to their original accounts. Accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.

Winning Margin

  • This refers to betting on the point difference between the 2 teams in the match. The winning margin is calculated with both teams starting at zero (0) points. For settlement purposes, the point difference at the end of the match is used. If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts. Accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.

Rugby Union Rules

  • Anytime try scorer
    • If the player is part of the match at any point in the 80-minute of play and any added time, bets will stand and will not be void.
    • For any abandoned or cancelled match, regardless of whether the selected player was a starter or not, all bets on that player will be void and bets will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the player is not part of the match at all, all singles bets will be void and refunded to their original accounts whilst accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • Home team winning margin
    • Refers to the point difference between the home team and the away team, with home team being the winner of the match. The winning margin is calculated with both teams starting on zero points.
  • Away team winning margin
    • Refers to the point difference between the away team and the home team, with the away team being the winner of the match. The winning margin is calculated with both teams starting on zero points.
  • Dozen winning margin
    • Refers to the point difference between the two (2) teams in the match. The winning margin is calculated with both teams starting on zero points.
  • First try scorer
    • Refers to the player whom will score the first try in the match. If the selected player is named in the squad, bets will stand for that player.
    • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time and the first try has already been scored prior to the abandonment, then all bets stand.
    • If no try has been scored at the time of abandonment, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the player enters the field of play after the first legal try has been scored, then this player is deemed a "non-runner" and all bets on this player are void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the match remains scoreless after normal and any extra time, then single wagers are void and wagers will be refunded to their original accounts whilst accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • Last try scorer
    • Refers to the player whom will score the last try in the match. If the selected player is named in the squad, bets will stand for that player.
    • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the player enters the field of play after the last legal try is scored, then all bets on this player are void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the match remains scoreless after normal and any extra time, then single wagers are void and wagers will be refunded to their original accounts whilst accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • Team to score first try
    • Refers to the team which will score the first try in the match.
    • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time and the first try has already been scored prior to the abandonment, then all bets stand.
    • If no try has been scored at the time of abandonment, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the match remains scoreless after normal and any extra time, then single wagers are void and wagers will be refunded to their original accounts whilst accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • Team to score last try
    • Refers to the team which will score the last try in the match.
    • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the match remains scoreless after normal and any extra time, then single wagers are void and wagers will be refunded to their original accounts whilst accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • Team to score first
    • Refers to the team which will score first in the match. This includes penalty tries.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time and a team has already scored first prior to the abandonment, then all bets stand.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time and no team has scored prior to the abandonment, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
    • If the match remains scoreless after normal and any extra time, then single wagers are void and wagers will be refunded to their original accounts whilst accumulative bets will be settled on the remaining selections.
  • Team to score last
    • Refers to the team which will score last in the match. This includes penalty tries.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
  • 1x2 (80 minutes)
    • Wagers can be made by either choosing a winner of the match or a tie.
    • If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
  • Asian handicap
    • Refers to the team which will win the match after the handicap values have been applied
    • If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void and will be refunded to their original accounts.
