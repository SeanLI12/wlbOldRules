New Features - August 2013

Black or White?

Prefer our classic look or maybe you'd like a change? No problem as now you can choose the colour theme!

  • Choose from 2 colour themes, our classic dark look or try our new lighter theme!
  • To choose your preferred colour theme, click on the "Shade" selector, which is located next to the "Contact Us" & language selection buttons.

Enhanced Left Navigation Panel

We've re-designed the left menu to offer you easier access to all your favourite sports & events!

  • Sports & all available competitions are laid out clearly in our new, easy to use left menu. Whether you want to view today's events, events that are In-Play or future events, it's all now just a click away.
  • Add or remove what you like most. You can easily add your favourite competitions via the "My Competitions" function. Just click on the stars located to the left of the competition name to add a competition to your list.
  • To view your selected competitions, click on the "View All" option under the "My Competitions" section on the left navigation menu.
  • To remove a competition, click on the star of the competition you'd like to remove or click on the large red "X" to reset your list.
  • We'll remember what you like so your favourite competitions are saved even if you exit our site! The next time you come back, your selected competitions will still be there.*

See More or Less Markets - The Choice is Yours!

Our site now detects your screen size & loads up available markets accordingly! The bigger your screen resolution, the more markets are loaded. Whether you prefer to see only the main lines or maybe you're more interested in multiple handicaps or periods bets, you can now customise which markets are loaded up based on your preferences!*

  • Want to see only the main markets? Click on the gear icon to bring up your settings and select "Main Handicap & O/U Line" under the "Number of Lines per Event" option.
  • Want to see alternative Asian Handicaps? Under the settings menu, select "View All" under the "Number of Lines per Event" option.
  • Want to see our period markets? You can click on the "1st Half"; "Halves"; "Quarters"; "Game Betting" or "Sets" buttons located just below the settings menu gear icon.

* The site uses cookies which are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile phone when you browse our site. These cookies help us to provide to you the best experience possible. All your preferences on our site are saved via cookies therefore, should you wish to remove or not use cookies from our site it will likely mean that our site will not function as you would expect.
