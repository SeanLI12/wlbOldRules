SNOOKER / 9-BALL TOUR

  • If a player fails to start a tournament or match, all bets on that player, or individual match, will be voided.
  • If a match is not completed for any reason then all bets will be voided.
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be voided.
  • Should a postponed match not resume within 12 hours from the original scheduled time all bets will be voided, unless otherwise stated.
  • If the match starts before schedule, only the transactions before the match starts will be considered valid. The transactions after the match starts will be considered invalid. (Except in-play bet types)
  • IN-PLAY HANDICAP
  • In-Play bets will be settled according to the match result after the match is finished.
  • To win: Handicap In-Play wagers win/loss is determined by the result of the final score deducting contemporary score at time of bet placement.
