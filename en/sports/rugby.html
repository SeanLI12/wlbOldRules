RUGBY UNION / RUGBY LEAGUE

Last Update: 03/11/2017

General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • Unless otherwise stated, all Rugby Union and Rugby League bets are settled based on the scheduled "80 minutes" play.
  • Regular time refers to "80 minutes" play and includes any stoppage time.
  • Matches do not include Extra Time unless otherwise stated in the bet type name.
  • All In Play bets will be settled based on the outcome at the end of the scheduled "80 minutes" play.
  • For International matches, as long as the change in venue is within same country, all bets are considered valid.
  • For 1st half betting, bets are settled based on the result derived from the 1st half of the match.
  • For 2nd half betting, bets are settled based on the result derived from the 2nd half of the match, including any extra time.
  • If a match is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets are considered void. If a match is suspended or abandoned during the 2nd half or during extra time, all 2nd half bets are considered void, unless otherwise stated in the individual bet type rules. All 1st half bets will still be valid.
Bet Types

Winner (Draw No Bet)

  • Predict who will be the match winner at the end of regular time.
  • If the result of the match at the end of regular time is a draw, then all bets will be refunded.

Winner (including Extra Time)

  • Predict who will be the match winner.
  • Regular time and Extra time are applicable to the result of this market.
  • If there is no winner after Extra Time and the result is declared a draw, then all bets will be refunded.

1 x 2

  • Predict which team will be the match winner or whether the match will end as a draw after regular time.

1 x 2 – 1st Half

  • Predict which team will be the match winner or whether the match will end as a draw after the 1st Half of regular time.

1 x 2 – 2nd Half

  • Predict who will be the match winner or whether the match will end as a draw after the 2nd Half of regular time.

Handicap

  • Predict the winner of the match with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Settlement is based on the final score at the end of regular time, including the stated handicap applied to each team at time of bet placement.

Handicap – 1st Half

  • Predict the winner of the 1st half of regular time with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Settlement is based on the final score at the end of the 1st half, including the stated handicap applied to each team at time of bet placement.

Handicap (including Extra Time)

  • Predict who will be the match winner.
  • Regular time and Extra time are applicable to the result of this market.
  • Settlement is based on the final score at the end of Extra Time (if required), including the stated handicap applied to each team at time of bet placement.

Total Points: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by both teams in regular time will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a match is abandoned at any stage, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no affect on the market result.

Total Points: Over / Under – 1st Half

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by both teams in the 1st half will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a match is abandoned in the 1st Half, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no effect on the market result.

Total Points 3-Way

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by both teams will be over, under or between the points range offered, at the end of regular time.
  • E.g. If the points range offered was 50-60, then ‘Under’ would be a winning bet if final score was 49 or less, ‘Over’ would be a winning bet if the final score was 61 or more and ‘Between’ would be a winning bet if the final score was anywhere between 50-60 (inclusive of both numbers).
  • If a match is abandoned at any stage, all bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no affect on the market result.

Team Points: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by the stated team will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a match is abandoned at any stage, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no affect on the market result.

Team Points: Over / Under – 1st Half

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by the stated team will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a match is abandoned in the 1st Half, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no effect on the market result.

Home Team Points: Over / Under – 1st Half

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by the home team will be over or under the indicated total line at the end of the 1st half of regular time.
  • If a match is abandoned in the 1st Half, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no effect on the market result.

Away Team Points: Over / Under – 1st Half

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored by the away team will be over or under the indicated total line at the end of the 1st half of regular time.
  • If a match is abandoned in the 1st Half, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no effect on the market result.

Try

  • Predict which team will score the next Try in the match.
  • If the match is abandoned before the stated Try is scored then all bets will be considered void.

Try Converted

  • Predict whether the last Try scored will be converted by the team who scored the Try.
  • If the referee orders the conversion to be retaken, then the result will be based on the next or further attempt deemed acceptable.
  • If the conversion is not attempted by the scoring team, then all bets will be considered void.
  • For Rugby Union only, if the Try is a Penalty Try, all bets on this market will be void.

Total Match Tries: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of match tries will be over or under the total line stated.
  • If a match is abandoned at any stage, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no affect on the market result.

Match Tries (Team): Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of match tries for the stated team (Home or Away) will be over or under the total line stated.
  • If a match is abandoned at any stage, all Over / Under bets will be considered void, unless the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points will have no affect on the market result.

Home Team to Score a Try

  • Predict whether the Home Team will score a single Try or more, at any point during regular time.

Away Team to Score a Try

  • Predict whether the Away Team will score a single Try or more, at any point during regular time.

Home Team to Score 2 or More Tries

  • Predict whether the Home Team will score a 2 Tries or more, at any point during regular time.

Away Team to Score 2 or More Tries

  • Predict whether the Away Team will score a 2 Tries or more, at any point during regular time.

Home Team to Score 3 or More Tries

  • Predict whether the Home Team will score a 3 Tries or more, at any point during regular time.

Away Team to Score 3 or More Tries

  • Predict whether the Away Team will score a 3 Tries or more, at any point during regular time.

Winning Margin (Point Bands)

  • Predict the winning margin for the winning team at the end of regular time.
  • Settlement will be based on the final scores recorded by both teams at the end of regular time.
  • The market may be offered in 3 different ways:
    • Variable Point Band, e.g. X-X points.
    • 10 Point Bands, e.g. 01-10, 11- 20, 21-30 etc.
    • 5 Point Bands, e.g. 01-5, 6-10, 11-15, 16-20 etc.
  • If the match is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be void.

First Try Scorer

  • Predict if the selection will score the first try in the game ("80 Minutes play" only)
  • If the player takes part in the match before the first try has been scored, bets will stand.
  • If the player takes to the field after the first try has been scored, all bets are considered void.
  • If the player does not take part in the match, all bets are considered void.

Last Try Scorer

  • Predict if the selection will score the last try in the game ("80 Minutes play" only)
  • If the player takes part in the match at any point during the scheduled "80 minutes" play, bets will stand.
  • If the player does not take part in the match, all bets are considered void.

Anytime Try Scorer

  • Predict if the selection will score a try at anytime during the game ("80 Minutes play" only).
  • If the player takes part in the match at any point during the scheduled "80 minutes" play, bets will stand.
  • If the player does not take part in the match, all bets are considered void.
  • If the match is abandoned after a player has scored, then all bets for that player within the "Anytime Try Scorer" market will be considered valid.

Team to Score First Try

  • Predict which team will score the first try in the game.
  • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and the first try has already been scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If no try has been scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If no try has been scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.

Team to Score Last Try

  • Predict which team will score the last try in the game.
  • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If no try has been scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.

Team to Score First

  • Predict which team will score first in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and a team has already scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If neither team has scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.

Team to Score Last

  • Predict which team will score last in the game.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.
