HANDBALL

Last Update: 03/11/2017

General Rules
  • Unless otherwise stated, all Handball bets will be settled based on the results of the full 60 minutes of play excluding overtime or penalty shootouts. This includes any additional stoppage time that maybe added at the end of the game.
  • If the game starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the game commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the game commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.
  • For any competition that uses a “Mercy Rule” all bets will stand according to the result at that time.
Bet Types

1 X 2

  • Predict who will win the game. This market will contain the two teams and the draw as betting selections.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Handicap (In-Play)

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line.

Over / Under (In-Play)

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a game is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential goals have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
  • Settlement is based on the final score line.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be odd or even.
