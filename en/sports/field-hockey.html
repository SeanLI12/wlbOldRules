FIELD HOCKEY

Last Update: 20/09/2016

General Rules
  • Outdoor Field Hockey bets will be settled on the basis of 4 X 15 minute periods.
  • First Half bets will be settled on the score at the end of Period 2 (i.e. "30 minutes" inc. stoppage time)
  • Indoor Field Hockey bets will be settled on the basis of 2 X 20 minute halves.
  • First Half bets will be settled on the score at the end of 1st Half (i.e. "20 minutes" inc. stoppage time)
  • For Quarter / Half betting, the period must be completed for bets to be valid.
  • If the match starts before the scheduled time, only bets placed before the match commenced will be considered valid. Bets placed after the match commenced will be considered void. This excludes In-Play bet types.
  • Overtime does not count unless otherwise stated.
Bet Types

1 X 2

  • Predict who will win the match / period. This market will contain the two teams and the draw as betting selections.

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the match / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Handicap (In-Play)

  • Predict who will win the match / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line for the match/ period.
  • If a match is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential goals have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.

Over / Under (In-Play)

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated total line for the game/ period.

Odd / Even

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be odd or even.
