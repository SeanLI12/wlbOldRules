BEACH SOCCER

Last Update: 20/09/2016

General Rules
  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • Unless otherwise stated, all Beach Soccer bets are settled based on the result at the end of three periods (12 minutes each).
  • If the match started earlier than the scheduled time, only bets before the match time will be accepted, otherwise bets will be considered void.
Bet Types

1 X 2

  • Predict who will win the match. This market will contain the two teams and the draw as betting selections

Handicap

  • Predict who will win the match / period with the indicated handicap applied.

Handicap (In-Play)

  • Predict who will win the match / period with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Settlement is based on the score line, from when the bet was placed to the end of the match / period - i.e. the final score minus the current score of the match.

Total Goals: Over / Under

  • Predict whether the total number of goals scored will be over or under the indicated goal line.
  • If a match is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined and any further goals have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void.
